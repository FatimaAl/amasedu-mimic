About Us

American Association for Continuous Education

(AACE) is an international organization that provides Continuous Medical Education programs, trainings, event management, and consultation for physicians. Our Services are offered globally and we are pioneers in providing international Continuous Medical Education.

American Association

theoretical and hands-on curriculum got the full accreditation from American Association of continuing medical education (AACME) and Dubai Health Authority (DHA). And we provide our attendees with accredited attendance and CME hour certificates.

Vision

Nationally and internationally acclaimed as leaders in professional continuous medical training.

Mission

providing quality-based learning programs that are geared toward helping physicians achieve their professional career goals with up-to-date knowledge and techniques, to enhance learners’ competence, practice performance, and patient satisfaction.

Objective

Provide high-quality CME programs and training that improve physicians’ competence and performance.
